Néfi Leite

When we are going to adopt a dog, we often simply think about how to feed it, and keep it clean and safe. But the truth is that a dog needs much more than that. After all, he is much more than a simple animal that needs to survive in the middle of a dense and dangerous forest. He is an animal that needs a home, a family, and lots of love. This need exists and you need to fill it. So, if you have any questions, type them in your message box, and we will respond and help you!
